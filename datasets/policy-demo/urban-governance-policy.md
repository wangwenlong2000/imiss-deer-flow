# Urban Governance Policy Demo

## Article 1
City traffic data collected for governance analysis shall be used for public-service decision support, emergency response, and infrastructure optimization.

## Article 2
Personally identifiable information should be minimized before data sharing or model training.

## Article 3
Street-view imagery used for urban renewal evaluation should preserve source metadata, acquisition time, and district scope.

## Article 4
Cross-domain analysis involving trajectory, traffic, imagery, and regulations must keep traceable dataset provenance.
